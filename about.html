This APP is based on the principles of Weather forecasting.It is weather related utility which was developed with the help of HTML,CSS,& JAVASCRIPT.This app is well sufficient to show the user all details related to the weather forecasting like humidity,wind_speed,temperature,timing related to sun,and etc of any city all over the world.

The Important part in this app is Search menu.By default the home is set to show the weather forcast of city Mumbai in Dyanamic Manner and at bottom There are some more common cities are show with respect to its details of weather.But This app will able to show weather details of any city which was present nationally aur internationally.You have to just type the city name in the search bar then click on the search button.Now, It will show the weather details of your entered city.which was approaxiametly 99% accurate.Be carefull while entering the city name It will only accept the correct name or valid existing city name.
